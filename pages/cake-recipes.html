CAKE RECIPES

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:
  • 200g butter
  • 300g self-raising flour
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 100g sugar
  • 300ml milk
  • 250g grated chocolate
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180째C (160째C fan).
  2. Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the flour, cocoa powder, and sugar; stir until smooth.
  3. Add milk and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the grated chocolate. Cool to room temperature and chill in the refrigerator.

Lemon Blueberry Layer Cake

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 and 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 Tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 Tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 and 1/2 cups blueberries
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (177째C). Grease and line three 9-inch round cake pans with parchment paper.
  2. Beat butter and sugars until creamy. Add eggs and vanilla; mix until combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the wet ingredients.
  4. Fold in buttermilk, lemon zest, and lemon juice, then gently fold in blueberries.
  5. Divide batter into pans and bake for 21-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
